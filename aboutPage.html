ABOUT US

Established in New York City over fifteen years ago, AlexKGM sneaker shop revolutionized sneaker retail as the original consignment store for rare shoes. Carrying the latest drops, coveted classics and hard-to-find grails, AlexKGM sneaker shop has evolved from a one-stop sneaker destination to a cultural hub for sneaker enthusiasts and novices alike.

With three brick-and-mortar locations in New York City, Los Angeles and Miami, AlexKGM sneaker shop remains the premier source for authentic, rare shoes.
